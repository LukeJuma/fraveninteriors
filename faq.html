Have Questions? We Have Answers

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of interior design and decoration services including space planning, furniture selection, color consultation, lighting design, and complete interior makeovers for residential and commercial spaces.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and size. A small room refresh typically takes 2-4 weeks, while larger residential projects may take 2-3 months. Commercial projects are assessed individually.

What is your design process?

Our process begins with a consultation to understand your vision and requirements. We then create design concepts, present mood boards and 3D visualizations, incorporate your feedback, and finally execute the design with attention to detail.

Do you work with all budgets?

Yes! We work with clients across all budget ranges. We'll discuss your budget upfront and create solutions that maximize value and impact within your financial parameters.

Can you work with an existing style?

Absolutely! Whether you want to refresh your current style or completely transform your space, we can adapt to your preferences and work within your existing decor to enhance and elevate it.

Do you provide furniture sourcing?

Yes! We source furniture and decor items from trusted suppliers and vendors. We handle the selection, ordering, and delivery coordination to ensure quality and consistency with your design vision.

Do you offer consultation services?

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual consultations. Whether you need a one-time design consultation or ongoing support, we're here to help guide you through your interior design journey.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us via phone, email, or our contact form. We'll schedule an initial consultation where we'll discuss your project, goals, and budget. From there, we'll create a customized plan for your space.

Still have questions?

Don't hesitate to reach out to us. Our team is ready to help!
